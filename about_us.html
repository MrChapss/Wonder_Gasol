WHO WE ARE

OUR COMPANY

Wonder Gasol is a Filipino home brewed Company focused on delivery and providing quality trusted gas cooking products to the whole Q.C region.


THE WONDER GASOL STORY

The Wonder Gasol company has continued to focus its goal to provide all Q.C. citizens and business owners for their own use. Starting in 2020 the goal was to expand business in the whole Q.C. region and provide them with quality and fairly priced gas products.

Mission

To deliver quality, and affordable gas to the Q.C. community and its population.

VISION

To be the no. 1 trusted and picked gas delivery business in the whole Q.C. region and be know to provide affordable best quality gas.
